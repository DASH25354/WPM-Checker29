Three-minute Training

【29】Black Friday is the Friday after Thanksgiving in the United States. It is the start of holiday shopping. Many stores have big sales and special deals. People line up early. Some buy in stores, and many buy online. The word “Black” can mean that stores move from losing money to making money. Over time, Black Friday spread to other countries, like Japan. Big chains also make sales there. Shoppers plan by checking prices, making a wish list, and reading ads. Stores plan by adding stock, staff, and safety steps to help large crowds. There can be long lines and some chaos, but many people now like online deals. Sales often start early and go on through the weekend to Cyber Monday. Black Friday is an important day for business and culture. It changes how stores sell and how people buy during the holiday season.
